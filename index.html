ABOUT OF WOLF MUSIC

Wolf Music is a young Argentine company founded in 2019 by four friends. We specialize in organizing concerts in various locations around the world. Our distinctive feature lies in our ability to bring true legends of rock and electronic music to the stage. Furthermore, we are venturing into the world of K-pop, a genre that, up to this point, had not graced our stages. We've had the honor of hosting giants such as Depeche Mode, Iron Maiden, AC/DC, among others. In the realm of electronic music, we've showcased renowned artists like Tiesto, KSHMR, David Guetta, and Alan Walker. On November 19, we return to our homeland with the successful band My Chemical Romance. We promise not only to create an unforgettable night for all fans of the band but also to showcase the grandeur of our shows, a distinguishing feature of our company.
