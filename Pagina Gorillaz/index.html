ABOUT OF GORILLAZ

Gorillaz is a British virtual band created in 1998 by Damon Albarn and Jamie Hewlett. The band is composed of four fictional members: 2-D, Noodle, Murdoc Niccals, and Russel Hobbs. Most of their songs, along with their entire fictional universe, are presented through animated music videos, traditional animation, and computer-generated animation, as well as interviews and short animated films. In reality, Damon Albarn is the only permanent member of the band. The producer and executive, Remi Kabaka Jr., began collaborating with the group in 2016 after providing the voice for Russel Hobbs for several years and was officially named a member alongside Albarn and Hewlett in 2019 in the Gorillaz documentary.
