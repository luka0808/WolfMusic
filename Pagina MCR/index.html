My Chemical Romance

I'm Not Okay

NEWS 19.11.2023

My Chemical Romance announced their return to the stage after six years apart. Gerard Way, Mikey Way, Frank Iero, Ray Toro and James Dewees will meet on November 19 of this year at the Shrine Auditorium & Expo Hall in Los Angeles, California. The big comeback show is titled Like Phantoms Forever and tickets, starting at almost $150, are on sale now. So far this is the only meeting announced by the band, although My Chemical Romance fans are already speculating about a new album and a world tour.

ABOUT of MCR

My Chemical Romance is an American rock band, formed in 2001 in the state of New Jersey, and integrated for most of its history by vocalist Gerard Way, bassist Mikey Way and guitarists Ray Toro and Frank Iero; also Matt Pelissier and Bob Bryar were once the drummers. In March 2013, the band announced their separation; although after almost seven years of inactivity, on October 31, 2019 they announced their reunion for a world tour that began the following December 20 in Los Angeles (California).
