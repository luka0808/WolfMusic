ABOUT OF DAVID GUETTA

In 2001, David Guetta made his debut in the music scene with the release of "Just a Little More Love," a single featuring American singer Chris Willis. This marked the beginning of a successful journey that led to his 2002 debut album of the same name, featuring hit tracks like "Love Don't Let Me Go" and "People Come, People Go." Over the years, Guetta continued to rise in the EDM world, releasing albums like "Guetta Blaster" in 2004 and "Pop Life" in 2007, with singles like "The World is Mine" and "Love Is Gone." However, it was with his 2009 album, "One Love," that he achieved global recognition, with chart-toppers such as "When Love Takes Over" and "Sexy Bitch." His subsequent album, "Nothing but the Beat" (2011), solidified his status as an EDM icon, featuring hits like "Where Them Girls At" and "Titanium." Guetta's impact on the electronic music scene has been significant, and he remains a celebrated figure in the industry.
