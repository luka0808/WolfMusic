ABOUT OF LINKIN PARK

Linkin Park, the American rock band from California, achieved global fame with their debut album "Hybrid Theory" in 2000, blending alternative rock, heavy metal, and hip hop elements. They continued to evolve their sound with subsequent albums, becoming one of the most successful rock acts in the 2000s. Tragically, the band went on hiatus following the death of their lead vocalist, Chester Bennington, in 2017, and they have not produced new music or planned tours since then. Linkin Park is renowned as one of the best-selling bands of the 21st century, having sold over 100 million records worldwide, and they have received numerous awards and accolades, solidifying their place in rock music history.
